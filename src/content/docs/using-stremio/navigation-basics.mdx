## Discover Page (Your Home)

The Discover page is what you see when you first open Stremio. It shows:

### Catalogs

Rows of content organized by:
- **Category** (Movies, TV Shows, Channels)
- **Genre** (Action, Comedy, Documentary, etc.)
- **Popularity** (Trending, Top Rated, Popular)
- **Source** (by add-on‚Äîe.g., "Trending on YouTube")

### How Discover Works

- **Content comes from your installed add-ons**
- More add-ons = more catalogs
- Catalogs update automatically when add-ons are added/removed

### Browsing Catalogs

- **Scroll horizontally** through each row
- **Click "See more"** to expand a row into a full page
- **Click any poster** to see details

<Callout type="note">
If your Discover page is empty, you need to install add-ons. See [Add-ons Explained](/addons/addons-explained/).
</Callout>

## Library Page (Your Collection)

Your Library has three tabs:

### üìñ Library

Content you've **actively added** to your collection:
- Click the **"+"** or bookmark icon on any title to add it here
- Useful for tracking what you want to watch

### üëÅÔ∏è Continue Watching

Shows and movies you're **currently watching**:
- Automatically added when you start watching something
- Shows your progress (e.g., "S02E05, 15 minutes remaining")
- Click to resume where you left off

### üìú History

**Everything you've watched**, in chronological order:
- Automatically tracked
- Can be cleared from Settings if needed

### Library Features

- **Sync across devices** (if you have an account)
- **Sort and filter** (by date added, title, etc.)
- **Remove items** (click the "..." menu on any title)

## Search Page

### How to Search

1. Click **Search** in sidebar, or
2. Use the search bar at the top (any page)

### What You Can Search For

- Movie titles
- TV show titles
- Actor names
- Director names
- YouTube channels (if YouTube add-on is installed)

### Search Results

- Results come from **all your installed add-ons**
- Click any result to see details
- If you don't see what you're looking for, check if you have relevant add-ons installed

<Callout type="tip">
**Search is only as good as your add-ons.** If you search for a movie and get no results, it means none of your installed add-ons have that movie in their catalogs.
</Callout>

## Content Detail Pages

When you click any movie or show, you'll see:

### Top Section

- **Poster/Backdrop**
- **Title and Release Year**
- **Rating** (from metadata add-ons)
- **Add to Library** button

### Tabs

**Overview:**
- Plot summary
- Cast and crew
- Runtime, genre, etc.

**Streams:** (most important tab)
- Available sources to watch from
- Click a stream to start playback

**Videos:** (for TV shows)
- Episode list organized by season
- Track which episodes you've watched

**Details:**
- Extended metadata (production companies, budget, etc.)

<Callout type="note">
**No streams available?** This means your add-ons don't have a source for this title. You may need to install different add-ons or the content simply isn't available.
</Callout>

## Add-ons Page

Manage your content sources here.

### Tabs

**Installed:**
- Add-ons you've already installed
- Click "Uninstall" to remove
- Click the add-on name to configure (if it has settings)

**Community Add-ons:**
- Curated list of popular add-ons
- Browse by category
- Click "Install" to add

**My Add-ons:** (if logged in)
- Add-ons you've installed on any device
- Synced across your account

### Installing Add-ons

- Click an add-on to see details
- Click "Install" button
- Confirm installation
- Add-on appears in your Installed tab

See [How to Install & Remove Add-ons Safely](/addons/how-to-install-remove-addons-safely/) for detailed guidance.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

Speed up your navigation with these shortcuts (desktop only):

### General Navigation

- **Home** ‚Äî Go to Discover
- **Ctrl + F** (Cmd + F on Mac) ‚Äî Focus search bar
- **Esc** ‚Äî Close current view/go back

### Player Controls

- **Spacebar** ‚Äî Play/Pause
- **F** ‚Äî Toggle fullscreen
- **M** ‚Äî Mute/Unmute
- **Arrow Up/Down** ‚Äî Volume up/down
- **Arrow Left/Right** ‚Äî Seek backward/forward 5 seconds
- **Ctrl + Left/Right** (Cmd on Mac) ‚Äî Seek backward/forward 30 seconds
- **S** ‚Äî Toggle subtitles
- **Shift + S** ‚Äî Cycle subtitle tracks
- **A** ‚Äî Cycle audio tracks

### Advanced

- **Ctrl + Shift + I** (Cmd + Option + I on Mac) ‚Äî Open developer tools (troubleshooting)

<Callout type="tip">
**Learn just 3 shortcuts to start:**  
1. **Spacebar** (play/pause)  
2. **F** (fullscreen)  
3. **Arrow keys** (seek/volume)
</Callout>

## Common Navigation Mistakes

### ‚ùå Looking for content in Library before adding it

**Why it's bad:** Library only shows what you've manually added.

**What to do instead:** Use Discover or Search to find content, then add it to Library.

### ‚ùå Expecting all content to appear automatically

**Why it's bad:** Discover only shows content from installed add-ons.

**What to do instead:** Install relevant add-ons first. See [Add-ons Explained](/addons/addons-explained/).

### ‚ùå Clicking the first stream without checking alternatives

**Why it's bad:** The first stream might be low quality or unreliable.

**What to do instead:** Click the "Streams" tab, review all available sources, and pick the best one.

### ‚ùå Not using search (browsing catalogs instead)

**Why it's bad:** Browsing is inefficient if you know what you want.

**What to do instead:** Use the search bar for specific titles.

## Customization Tips

### Organize Your Library

- Add only content you actually want to track
- Use Continue Watching for in-progress items
- Clear History periodically if it gets cluttered (Settings ‚Üí Advanced ‚Üí Clear cache)

### Manage Your Add-ons

- Uninstall add-ons you don't use (reduces clutter on Discover page)
- Organize by priority (can't reorder, but you can uninstall/reinstall in preferred order)

### Adjust Player Settings

- Set preferred subtitle size and language in Settings
- Enable hardware acceleration if playback is choppy
