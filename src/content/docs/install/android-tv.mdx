## Method 1: Google Play Store (Easiest)

If your device has Google Play Store:

<Steps>

1. **Open Google Play Store** on your Android TV

2. **Search for "Stremio"** using the TV remote

3. **Select the Stremio app** by Stremio

4. **Click "Install"**

5. **Wait for installation**, then click "Open"

</Steps>

---

## Method 2: Sideload APK (Fire TV, non-Google devices)

<Callout type="warning">
Sideloading requires enabling Developer Options. Only proceed if you're comfortable with this.
</Callout>

<Steps>

1. **Enable Developer Options**
   - Go to **Settings** → **My Fire TV** (or **Device**)
   - Click **About**
   - Click **Build Number** 7 times until you see "You are now a developer"

2. **Enable ADB Debugging and Apps from Unknown Sources**
   - Go to **Settings** → **My Fire TV** → **Developer Options**
   - Enable **ADB Debugging**
   - Enable **Apps from Unknown Sources**

3. **Install Downloader app**
   - Search for **"Downloader"** in the app store
   - Install it

4. **Download Stremio APK**
   - Open **Downloader**
   - Enter URL: `https://www.stremio.com`
   - Navigate to downloads and get the Android TV APK

5. **Install the APK**
   - Click **"Install"** when prompted
   - Wait for installation

6. **Disable Unknown Sources** (recommended)

</Steps>

---

## Important: Use Android TV Version

Make sure you download the **Android TV** version, not the phone/tablet version. The TV version has:
- TV-optimized interface
- Remote control support
- Better performance on TV hardware
