Login and sync issues can prevent you from accessing your Stremio account and data across devices. This guide covers the most common login problems and sync failures with step-by-step solutions.

## Login Problems

### Can't Log In

**Common Login Issues:**
- **Wrong Credentials**: Incorrect email or password
- **Account Not Verified**: Email not confirmed
- **Account Suspended**: Violation of terms of service
- **Server Issues**: Stremio servers down
- **Network Problems**: Connection blocking login

<Steps>

1. **Check Credentials**
   - Verify email address spelling
   - Try password reset if forgotten
   - Check for caps lock or auto-correct errors
   - Try logging in from web.stremio.com first

2. **Verify Account Status**
   - Check email for verification link
   - Resend verification email if needed
   - Contact support if account suspended

3. **Network Troubleshooting**
   - Try different internet connection
   - Disable VPN temporarily
   - Check firewall/antivirus settings
   - Clear browser cache and cookies

</Steps>

### Password Reset Issues

<Steps>

1. **Request Password Reset**
   - Click "Forgot Password" on login screen
   - Enter your account email address
   - Check spam/junk folder for reset email
   - Use the reset link within 24 hours

2. **Reset Email Not Received**
   - Check spam/junk folders thoroughly
   - Add stremio.com to email whitelist
   - Try different email address if multiple
   - Contact support with account details

3. **Reset Link Problems**
   - Copy full URL from email
   - Try different browser or device
   - Disable browser extensions temporarily
   - Check if link has expired

</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
Never share your password reset link with anyone. Legitimate Stremio support will never ask for your password.
</Callout>

### Account Verification Issues

**Email Verification Problems:**
- **Email Bounced**: Invalid email address
- **Spam Filtered**: Verification email blocked
- **Expired Link**: Verification link timed out
- **Already Verified**: Account already active

**Solutions:**
- Use a valid, accessible email address
- Check all email folders including spam
- Request new verification email
- Contact support if issues persist

## Sync Problems

### Sync Not Working

**Sync Failure Causes:**
- **No Account**: Not logged into Stremio account
- **Network Issues**: Poor or blocked internet connection
- **Server Problems**: Stremio sync servers down
- **Large Data**: Too much data to sync
- **Device Limits**: Exceeded device limit

<Steps>

1. **Verify Login Status**
   - Check if logged in (profile icon shows account)
   - Re-login if session expired
   - Verify account is active and in good standing

2. **Check Network Connection**
   - Test internet connectivity
   - Try different network (Wi-Fi vs. mobile data)
   - Disable VPN or proxy
   - Check firewall settings

3. **Force Sync**
   - Settings → Account → Force Sync
   - Or logout and login again
   - Wait 5-10 minutes for sync to complete
   - Check other devices for data

</Steps>

### Data Not Syncing

**What Should Sync:**
- Library and watchlist
- Viewing progress and history
- Add-on installations
- User preferences and settings

**Common Issues:**
- **Partial Sync**: Some data syncs, other doesn't
- **Old Data**: Changes not appearing on other devices
- **Missing Items**: Content added on one device missing elsewhere
- **Progress Lost**: Viewing progress not saved

<Steps>

1. **Check Sync Status**
   - Look for sync indicator in app
   - Check "Last synced" time in settings
   - Verify all devices show same account

2. **Manual Sync Trigger**
   - Make a small change (add item to library)
   - Wait for automatic sync
   - Check if change appears on other devices
   - Force sync if needed

3. **Data Verification**
   - Compare data across devices
   - Check for conflicting changes
   - Resolve duplicates or conflicts
   - Restore from backup if available

</Steps>

## Account Recovery

### Forgotten Email Address

<Steps>

1. **Check Associated Emails**
   - Try all email addresses you own
   - Check social media for Stremio mentions
   - Look for Stremio emails in inbox
   - Check payment receipts if applicable

2. **Contact Support**
   - Email support@stremio.com
   - Provide as much account information as possible
   - Include device information and approximate signup date
   - Be patient for response (may take days)

</Steps>

### Account Locked or Suspended

**Lock Reasons:**
- **Security**: Suspicious activity detected
- **Terms Violation**: Breach of service terms
- **Payment Issues**: Failed payment or fraud
- **Too Many Attempts**: Multiple failed login attempts

<Steps>

1. **Check Lock Reason**
   - Look for email from Stremio about lock
   - Check account status on web.stremio.com
   - Review terms of service violations

2. **Unlock Account**
   - Follow instructions in lock notification email
   - Complete security verification
   - Contact support if no email received
   - Provide identification if requested

</Steps>

## Device Management Issues

### Too Many Devices

**Device Limits:**
- **Free Accounts**: Unlimited devices
- **Premium Accounts**: Unlimited devices
- **Shared Accounts**: May have restrictions

<Steps>

1. **Check Device List**
   - Settings → Account → Manage Devices
   - See all devices linked to account
   - Identify unknown or unused devices

2. **Remove Old Devices**
   - Select device to remove
   - Click "Remove" or "Logout"
   - Confirm removal
   - Device loses access immediately

3. **Device Recognition**
   - Each device has unique identifier
   - Name devices for easy identification
   - Remove devices you no longer own

</Steps>

### Device Not Syncing

**Device-Specific Issues:**
- **Offline Device**: Device not connected to internet
- **App Version**: Outdated Stremio version
- **Platform Issues**: Device platform problems
- **Storage Full**: No space for sync data

<Steps>

1. **Check Device Status**
   - Verify device is online
   - Check Stremio app version
   - Ensure sufficient storage space
   - Test other apps' internet connectivity

2. **Reconnect Device**
   - Logout and login again
   - Restart Stremio app
   - Restart device completely
   - Check for app updates

</Steps>

## Network and Connectivity Issues

### VPN and Proxy Problems

**VPN Sync Issues:**
- **Blocked Servers**: VPN blocking Stremio servers
- **IP Changes**: Dynamic IP causing authentication issues
- **Slow Connection**: VPN latency affecting sync
- **Geographic Restrictions**: Regional blocking

<Steps>

1. **Test Without VPN**
   - Temporarily disable VPN
   - Try login and sync
   - Identify if VPN is the cause

2. **VPN Configuration**
   - Add Stremio domains to VPN whitelist
   - Use different VPN server
   - Change VPN protocol
   - Contact VPN provider for Stremio support

</Steps>

### Firewall and Security Software

**Blocking Issues:**
- **Antivirus**: Security software blocking connection
- **Firewall**: Network firewall rules
- **Browser Extensions**: Extensions interfering
- **Corporate Networks**: Workplace restrictions

<Steps>

1. **Check Security Software**
   - Temporarily disable antivirus
   - Add Stremio to antivirus exceptions
   - Check firewall rules
   - Whitelist Stremio domains

2. **Network Administrator**
   - Contact IT department for corporate networks
   - Request Stremio access
   - Use personal hotspot if allowed

</Steps>

## Advanced Troubleshooting

### Cache and Data Reset

<Steps>

1. **Clear App Cache**
   - Settings → Storage → Clear Cache
   - Don't clear all data (preserves login)
   - Restart app after clearing

2. **Selective Data Reset**
   - Clear sync cache only
   - Keep user data intact
   - Re-sync from server

3. **Complete Reset (Last Resort)**
   - ⚠️ Backup important data first
   - Clear all app data
   - Reinstall app
   - Login and restore data

</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
Clearing all data will remove local content. Backup your library and settings before resetting.
</Callout>

### Server Status Checking

**Check Service Status:**
- **Stremio Status Page**: Check for outages
- **Community Forums**: User reports of issues
- **Social Media**: Official Stremio updates
- **Downdetector**: User-reported problems

### Log Analysis

<Steps>

1. **Enable Debug Logging**
   - Settings → Advanced → Debug Logs
   - Reproduce login/sync issue
   - Check log files for error messages

2. **Common Error Codes**
   - **401**: Authentication failed
   - **403**: Access forbidden
   - **500**: Server error
   - **Network Timeout**: Connection issues

</Steps>

## Account Security

### Suspicious Activity

**Security Signs:**
- **Unknown Devices**: Devices you don't recognize
- **Failed Attempts**: Multiple login failures
- **Password Changes**: Unauthorized changes
- **Unusual Locations**: Logins from unexpected places

<Steps>

1. **Secure Account**
   - Change password immediately
   - Enable two-factor authentication
   - Review and remove unknown devices
   - Check recent account activity

2. **Report Suspicious Activity**
   - Contact Stremio support
   - Provide details of suspicious activity
   - Change password from trusted device
   - Monitor account for further issues

</Steps>

### Two-Factor Authentication

<Steps>

1. **Enable 2FA**
   - Settings → Account → Security
   - Choose authentication method (app or SMS)
   - Set up backup codes
   - Test 2FA login

2. **2FA Troubleshooting**
   - Check time sync on device
   - Ensure auth app is updated
   - Use backup codes if needed
   - Contact support for locked accounts

</Steps>

**When to Contact Support:**
- Account completely inaccessible
- Security concerns or suspicious activity
- Issues affecting all your devices
- Problems persisting after all troubleshooting

**Support Information:**
- **Email**: support@stremio.com
- **Response Time**: 24-48 hours typically
- **Required Info**: Account email, device details, error messages
- **Language**: English primary, some multilingual support

## Prevention Strategies

### Account Maintenance

✅ **Do:**
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Regularly review connected devices
- Keep recovery email accessible
- Monitor account activity
- Use trusted networks for login
- Backup important data

❌ **Don't:**
- Share account credentials
- Use public computers for login
- Ignore security notifications
- Use easily guessable passwords
- Leave accounts logged in on shared devices
- Ignore password change notifications
- Use untrusted Wi-Fi for account changes

### Sync Best Practices

**Maintain Sync Health:**
- Keep app updated on all devices
- Use stable internet connections
- Don't make conflicting changes simultaneously
- Regularly check sync status
- Clean up old/unused devices
- Monitor storage space
- Test sync periodically
